AI resume shortlisting uses artificial intelligence, natural language processing (NLP), and machine learning to automatically scan, parse, and rank resumes against job requirements, improving efficiency, accuracy, and reducing bias in the hiring process. The AI extracts key information like skills and experience, matches it to the job description, and generates a ranked shortlist of qualified candidates for human recruiters to review, ultimately speeding up the time-to-hire.
